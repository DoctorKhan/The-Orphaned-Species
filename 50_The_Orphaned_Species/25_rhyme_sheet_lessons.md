# Rhyme Sheet — the Lessons as connective tissue

*The thematic tier of the cross-era rhyme. Each Lesson of the Living Way is the **antidote to a specific Machiavellian trap** — and each trap recurs across the eras while its Lesson is recovered (or, in Adapa's case, failed). Companion to `20_resonance_points.md` (the object/sound carriers) and `10_triangulation_of_control.md` (the engine).*

---

## How this differs from the Resonance Points

Two kinds of thing rhyme across the eras, and they live in two docs:

- **Object/sound carriers** — the Wheel, the Heat, the Song, the Fox-spiral. Tangible things that recur. → `20_resonance_points.md`.
- **The Lessons** — the *thematic* echoes. The trap the Igigi priest sets at Göbekli is the same trap the chariot-king sets in the Bronze Age, and (per **Option A**) the same trap the reader recognizes in their own world. The Lesson is the antidote that recurs against it. → *this doc.*

**Option A is locked**, so the "present" column is **not** a modern Aedan with a boss and a clinic. The present face of each trap lands two ways: (1) the **reader's own recognition** — the book trusts you to see the corporate/algorithmic version without being told; and (2) a faint, *meaning-stripped* echo in the **Modern Child** coda (ch18). Aedan stays the Bronze-Age cave anchor; he never narrates a modern scene.

**The craft rule (non-negotiable):** the Lesson is *never spoken as a lesson in-scene.* It is **enacted** by an ancestor, against a trap, and named only in `Lessons_of_the_Living_Way.md`. The moment a character recites the moral, it curdles into a sermon. Per the Triangulation doc: write them as **anti-hacking protocols**, not homilies.

## The pairings worth building around

A few of these aren't just neat — they're structural spines:

- **Lesson 3 ↔ Adapa (the failure) ↔ Aedan (the payoff).** This is the book's deepest rhyme. Adapa *doesn't* know who he really is (he believes the Qingu-lie), so he locks himself (ch9). Aedan *does* (the stone warms, ch17). Same Lesson, the two ends of the book — the before-picture and the answer. See `30_beatsheet_ch09_adapa.md`.
- **Lesson 13 ↔ Anthea (refusal) vs. Adapa (failure).** Anthea is offered elevation-by-becoming-elite and refuses; Adapa accepts the lie that his worth is forfeit. Stage them as deliberate mirrors — the human who outgrew the parent vs. the human who couldn't.
- **Lesson 4 ↔ the Heat carrier.** Lesson 4's deeper reading says awakening "makes you **warmer**… compassion is the signature." That is *literally* the somatic spine of `20_resonance_points.md` (Carrier 3). The moral layer and the physical carrier are the same thing seen twice — kindness and the warm pole of the sensory zoning are one. Let Ila's healing heat *be* Lesson 4 without ever saying so.

## Rules for extending this sheet

1. **One trap, one antidote, recurring.** A Lesson earns a row only if its trap recurs across eras. If it's era-bound, it's texture, not a rhyme.
2. **Enact, never recite.** (See the craft rule above.) The reader does the naming.
3. **Present = recognition, not narration.** Per Option A, the modern column is what the *reader* sees in their own world plus the stripped echo in ch18 — not a modern Aedan scene.
4. **Adapa is the inverted row.** Where the Seven *recover* their Lesson, Adapa *fails* his (Lesson 3) — the control case that makes the recoveries mean something.
