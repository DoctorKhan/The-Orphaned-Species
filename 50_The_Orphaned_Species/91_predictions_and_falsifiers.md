# Testable Predictions & Falsifiers

*Companion to `00_MASTER_TIMELINE.md` and `90_epigraphs_and_sources.md`. The timeline separates the **anchor** (cited, mainstream science) from the **cave's gloss** (the claim beyond the anchor). This doc collects the glosses as **hypotheses** — wherever the framework claims more than current science, that surplus is a prediction future data can confirm or kill. "Assuming the cosmology is true" = "assuming these glosses resolve in its favour."*

*Each entry is tagged for epistemic status:*
- **[LIVE]** — genuinely open; current data is silent, partial, or contested. The framework is making a real bet.
- **[STRAINED]** — current data already leans against it; survives only on a narrow or retreated reading.
- **[UNFALSIFIABLE]** — by construction generates no clean test (directed-vs-undirected leaves identical traces; intent isn't a measurement).

*Rule, kept from the master timeline: cite the anchor confidently; flag the prediction as ours. A prediction is only worth listing if you can name what would **kill** it.*

---

## 1. Functional Loading — the deepest "designed vs. evolved" signature

*A designed intervention should be non-random and purposive; drift leaves junk. The sharpest test of the whole framework: are its claimed interventions functionally loaded?*

### 1.1 The chromosome-2 fusion seam is functionally live, not inert scar — **[LIVE]**
- **Claim (gloss):** the seam at 2q13–q14.1 is "firewall + receiver + lock-vault" (Phase II), not dead structural tissue.
- **Test:** functional genomics of the seam — enhancer activity, methylation-sensitive switches, trans-effects on brain/immune-expressed genes.
- **Falsifier:** the seam is a pure structural scar with no regulatory role.
- **Anchor:** the fusion site is a real, definitive head-to-head fusion (stranded TTAGGG telomeric repeats, degenerate centromere); chr-2 genuinely carries brain-development and immune genes. *Gloss:* that the seam is an active switch rather than inert.

### 1.2 Gigaviral "orphan" genomes are coherent toolkits, not noise — **[LIVE]**
- **Claim (gloss):** the *Pandoravirus / Pithovirus* orphan code is the seed-craft "hard drive" (Phase 0).
- **Test:** comparative phylogenomics — do orphan genes encode usable biosynthetic/structural capability? Do any foundational eukaryotic genes trace to a gigaviral source?
- **Falsifier:** orphan genes are fast-evolving non-functional junk with no downstream lineage.
- **Anchor:** giant viruses really do carry millions of base pairs of uncharacterised genes (Philippe et al. 2013). *Gloss:* that they are a deliberately-packed library.

### 1.3 Archaic introgression carries a selected brain-regulatory payload — **[STRAINED]**
- **Claim (gloss):** Neanderthal/Denisovan "wild code" tripped the dormant receiver and woke the mind (Phase III Activation).
- **Test:** scan introgressed segments overlapping HARs and brain eQTLs for positive selection.
- **Already leans against:** Reich-lab data shows archaic *regulatory* alleles were largely selected **against**, and Reich favours cultural innovation for the ~70 kya expansion. The framework's retreat to "cultural innovation as the cave's image for suddenness" concedes this — but the concession weakens the Activation as a *genetic* event.
- **Falsifier (already partly realised):** no introgressed brain-regulatory alleles under positive selection; the cognitive switch is unsupported at the sequence level.

## 2. Synchrony — "two independent events are secretly one act"

*The most elegant and most falsifiable class: each is a clean coincidence-vs-causation question. The framework claims a single hidden cause behind events mainstream science dates independently.*

### 2.1 Fusion (~0.9 Mya) ≡ the bottleneck (~930–813 kya) — **[LIVE]**
- **Claim (gloss):** one act of the Hardware Recall — the fusion performed *on* the bottlenecked founders (Phase II).
- **Test:** refine the dating of both; confirm the fusion arose *within* the bottlenecked founder population.
- **Falsifier:** the fusion substantially pre- or post-dates the bottleneck, or arose in a different lineage.
- **Anchor:** fusion re-dated ~0.9 Mya (Poszewiecka 2022; IJdo 1991); bottleneck to ~1,280 at ~930–813 kya (Hu et al. 2023 — itself contested). The framework is betting on a contested bottleneck.

### 2.2 Basal Eurasian release ≡ Neolithic onset — **[LIVE]**
- **Claim (gloss):** the sealed Qingu reserve, deployed on cue to carry agriculture (Eden II / Phase VI), plus an immune/metabolic "Lullu Patch."
- **Test (decisive):** recover an actual Basal Eurasian genome (currently a statistical ghost). The framework predicts it shows (a) *deeper* Neanderthal depletion than modelled, (b) a *sudden* ~10–12 kya entry rather than gradual gene flow, and (c) adaptive immune/metabolic alleles that sweep early farmers.
- **Falsifier:** a recovered genome looks like an ordinary hunter-gatherer with gradual admixture and no deployment signature.
- **Anchor:** Basal Eurasian is real, mainstream paleogenetics (Lazaridis 2014/2016), genuinely Neanderthal-depleted and concentrated in the first farmers. *Gloss:* isolation-as-holding-pen and appearance-as-release.

### 2.3 Y-diversity rebound (~4 kya) ≡ the 4.2-ky collapse — **[LIVE]**
- **Claim (gloss):** the controllers' grip releasing as the masters die/strand (Phase VII).
- **Test:** high-resolution dating of the male-line rebound against social-collapse horizons.
- **Falsifier:** the rebound is decoupled in time from collapse, or fully explained by Indo-European (Yamnaya/Sintashta) patrilineal expansion alone.
- **Anchor:** Karmin et al. 2015 document both the trough and rebound but pin neither to a single cause. *Gloss:* the clean "controllers leave → diversity rebounds" reading.

## 3. Astrobiology — the "parts catalogue shipped pre-activation"

### 3.1 Pristine sample-returns keep yielding the *complete* kit, persistently racemic — **[LIVE]**
- **Claim (gloss):** the architects salted the Solar System with the full abiotic toolkit *before* any biological handedness was switched on (Phase 0).
- **Test:** every future sample-return (more Bennu/Ryugu analysis, future comet/asteroid missions). The framework predicts continued discovery of the full amino-acid alphabet, all bio-essential sugars, nucleobases, cofactors, and cryoprotectants — and **no inherited homochirality**.
- **Falsifier:** pristine extraterrestrial organics show *biological* homochirality (a different model — actual biology in space), or only a sparse, incomplete set inconsistent with a "catalogue."
- **Anchor:** Bennu's near-perfect racemic (~50/50) amino acids, ~15/20 protein amino acids incl. tryptophan, abundant ammonia, ribose + glucose (Glavin et al. 2025; Furukawa et al. 2025). Pseudo-panspermia (building blocks from space) is respectable; *directed* is the gloss.

### 3.2 Trehalose or its pathway intermediates turn up in carbonaceous material — **[LIVE, soft]**
- **Claim (gloss):** the dragons' antifreeze toolkit is primordial space chemistry, assembled locally.
- **Test:** deeper organic analysis of returned samples.
- **Current state:** only the monomer (glucose) and the cryoprotectant urea are confirmed; trehalose itself has *not* been found in space.
- **Falsifier:** none in principle (absence isn't disproof), so keep this as supportive-if-found, not load-bearing.

## 4. Deep Biosphere — the dormant dragons

### 4.1 A deep-dwelling vertebrate-grade lineage leaves anomalous eDNA — **[STRAINED]**
- **Claim (gloss):** K-Pg reptilian survivors, vitrified and asleep in the Abzu / deep ice (Phase 0); "some may still be down there."
- **Test:** environmental-DNA surveys of deep caves, subglacial lakes (Vostok-type), deep ice cores, subterranean voids.
- **Current state:** null — only microbial extremophiles. Genuinely falsifiable and presently unsupported.
- **Falsifier:** comprehensive deep-environment eDNA continues to show nothing beyond microbial life.

### 4.2 A biochemical route to *large-body* vitrified cryostasis exists — **[STRAINED]**
- **Claim (gloss):** armored scales as thermal-regulation rig, "fire" as rapid-thaw, allowing freeze/revive at multi-ton scale.
- **Test:** cryobiology — any mechanism to vitrify and revive large tissue volumes without ice-crystal shear.
- **Current state:** **scale is the known killer** — small animals (wood frog, tardigrade) vitrify and revive; no known mechanism scales to a large body. Leans against.
- **Falsifier:** a confirmed hard thermodynamic limit on large-body vitrification.

## 5. Archaeology

### 5.1 Younger Dryas as manipulated climate rupture — **[REVISED; impact non-load-bearing]**
- **Claim (gloss):** the golden age ends when the Igigi sabotage the Anunnaki orbital/interface system. The Younger Dryas is the visible climate rupture caused or amplified by that sabotage; the later or staged loss of orbital hardware is the mythic "fall from heaven."
- **Test:** Younger Dryas paleoclimate remains a real abrupt event; evidence continues to support major ocean-atmosphere reorganization, meltwater routing, volcanic/geochemical anomalies, rain-belt shifts, and regional ecological stress.
- **Falsifier:** there is no need to defend Younger Dryas impact/airburst signatures. If platinum spikes, microspherules, or nanodiamonds fail replication, the cosmology does not fail; only an obsolete impact version fails.
- *Note:* This is a deliberate guardrail. The Younger Dryas impact hypothesis has become too weak and too contested to carry the cosmology. Use it only as an in-world rumor, factional misreading, or discarded theory.

### 5.2 Targeted-purge aDNA shows cross-kin selection, not local raiding — **[LIVE]** *(the sharp one)*
- **Claim (gloss):** the gendered massacres are bloodline purges hunting the hidden carriers (Phase IX–X), screened at the stone sites.
- **Test:** victim-vs-context aDNA + isotopes at more massacre sites.
- **Prediction (two tiers):** (a) victims recurrently *gathered across kin groups, mostly female* (already seen at Gomolava: unrelated, multi-settlement, ~87% female); (b) **the risky version** — victims share a cryptic genetic component absent in the perpetrators (the "carrier" signature).
- **Falsifier:** victims are ordinary local kin groups with no distinguishing ancestry — i.e. ordinary raiding/sacrifice.
- **Anchor:** Pömmelte (~2300 BCE) and Gomolava (~800 BCE) are real sites with the gendered-killing pattern. *Gloss:* that the selection criterion is a hunted lineage.

### 5.3 Göbekli's burial was deliberate and synchronous — **[LIVE]**
- **Claim (gloss):** decommissioning — a node powered down on schedule (Phase VI/VII).
- **Test:** site stratigraphy — intentional backfill vs. slow natural infill, and how synchronous across the enclosures.
- **Falsifier:** gradual, unplanned sedimentary infill.

### 5.4 Megaliths correlate with geophysical anomalies and have resonant properties — **[LIVE, weak]**
- **Claim (gloss):** High Places are grid-nodes sited on geomagnetic anomaly/stability; the "ancient internet" (Phase VI).
- **Test:** spatial statistics of major node placement vs. geomagnetic/geological anomaly maps; materials-acoustics of selected stones.
- **Toe-hold:** the Stonehenge bluestones' documented lithophonic (ringing) properties.
- **Falsifier:** siting fully explained by topography, astronomy, and resource access, with no anomaly correlation.
- *Note:* this sits on the same purely-mythic footing as the ley-line / crop-circle layer — the weakest-anchored zone in the timeline; cite as the cave's cosmology, not a science claim.

### 5.5 Near-total local male-line turnovers recur at "cage-rebuild" horizons — **[LIVE]**
- **Claim (gloss):** orphaned humans re-running the Igigi reproductive clamp themselves (Phase IX).
- **Test:** aDNA for Iberian-style events elsewhere — high autosomal continuity with near-total Y replacement.
- **Anchor (hard):** Iberia ~2500–2000 BCE, ~40% ancestry / ~100% Y turnover (Olalde et al. 2019). The framework predicts this is a *pattern*, not a one-off.
- **Falsifier:** Iberia stays anomalous; no comparable male-line wipeouts at other consolidation horizons.

## 6. Selection — "the cage rewrites the hardware"

### 6.1 Post-Neolithic directional selection intensified on survival-of-the-cage loci — **[LIVE → supported]**
- **Claim (gloss):** the Social Game itself becomes the selective filter (Phase IX).
- **Test / anchor:** large ancient-DNA selection scans (Akbari et al. 2026; ~16k West-Eurasian genomes) — already supporting intensified selection on immune, metabolic, inflammatory, disease-risk loci.
- **Falsifier:** selection shown to *slow* after farming (the older assumption).

### 6.2 Selection on cognition/behaviour — **[STRAINED — do not lean on]**
- **Claim (gloss):** "the prison selected its inmates."
- **Why strained:** polygenic scores for cognition derive from modern GWAS, don't transport across millennia/populations, and are badly confounded by structure and migration. **Keep strictly as mythic gloss; never present population-level cognitive evolution as established.** This is a live, easily-misused debate and the most reputationally dangerous claim in the set.

## 7. The Unfalsifiable Floor

*The deepest layer generates **no** clean predictions, by construction — and this is not a defect to hide but the doc's own thesis turned on itself.*

- **Directed vs. undirected panspermia leave identical physical traces.** "Intent behind the seeding" is not a measurement. Every §3 anchor is equally consistent with ordinary astrochemistry.
- **The anti-entropic medium and ley-line carrier** are unrecognised in mainstream physics; parapsychology's null, unreplicated record and the lack of evidence for physical ley lines stand against them as literal mechanisms. They belong to the book's cosmology, not its science-facing evidence.
- **The Tree-of-Life bridge / spirit-to-spirit plane** is defined as the *non-mediated* register — the one place, by its own logic, that leaves no transmittable trace.

**The mechanisms predict; the maker never will.** This is the Two Trees in epistemology: the mediated, entropic plane (genetics, chemistry, archaeology) is exactly the one where you can only ever read traces; the source can never be checked directly. A reader — like the orphaned species — is left to decide between strategist and scavenger on the evidence of traces alone. That undecidability is designed, not a gap to be closed.
